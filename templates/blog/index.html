F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do i get any certificate for participating or presenting in the conference?

    Yes, absolutely. You would recieve a certificate once the conference is done.

  • When will i recieve my certificate?

    Once the conference is done an automated verification process takes pace which in return would send your certificate through the user's registered email.

  • How do we keep ourselves informed about progress?

    We will send the user necessary updates to the registered email. We use a combination of pre-determined update schedules (normally email).

  • What happens if there is a schedule change?

    At times it might be necessary to cancel or reschedule the conference after receipt of a confirmation letter. The team will provide advance notice of any changes.

  • Why should I pay participation fees? Isn’t it too expensive?

    Our events are well designed and particularly organized to bring senior level management and key decision makers in the industry under one roof. Networking with such professionals at our event does not only give you easy access to have one on one meeting but also to benefit from their vast experience and diverse industry knowledge rather than free events whose presentations are mostly sales pitch and related to particular services or solutions.
